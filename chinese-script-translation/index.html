Why is script translation special?

Translating a voice over script is somewhat different from general document translation, because what you translate will not be directly read by people, but actually heard by the target audience. You must keep this in mind, and think about how the voice over artist will read the script and what tone of voice she/he will use.

Not so good script translations can confuse the VO artist and significantly affect his/her performance, and eventually, ruin the voice over project.

Script translation samples:

Case 1: Script translation and voice over for a Turkish document

Original:

“Bursa is the fourth largest city of Turkey. It is located in the Marmara region, and occupies an area of 11087 km2 with a population of 2.5 million people.”

Translation:

“布尔萨是土耳其的第四最大城市，位于马尔马拉地区，占据 11087 平方公里，人口超过 2500 000 人， 有历史价值， 同时也是土耳其的最大工业中心之一.”

Case 2: Script translation and voice over for an American EB-5 immigration program

Original:

“Thank you for your interest in the EB-5 Immigrant Investor Pilot Program. Maryland Area Regional Center LLC (“MARC LLC”) is here to assist you in finding the proper investment for your needs.”

Chinese Translation:

“感谢您对EB-5投资移民试点计划的兴趣。马里兰地区区域中心有限责任公司助您投资有道。”
